Welcome to the Festival!

Join us for our annual Natsumatsuri (Summer Festival) at Temple University, Japan Campus! This is a special day for us to celebrate the vibrant spirit of summer with traditional Japanese festivities.

We warmly welcome everyone—TUJ students, Setagaya locals, and our friends from Showa Women's University—to come together for an unforgettable evening of fun, food, and culture. Let's create wonderful summer memories together!
